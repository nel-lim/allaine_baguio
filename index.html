OUR LITTLE CAFÉ

One of the Philippines’ most beloved Café, Café Maria has welcomed guests to enjoy its exquisite coffee blends, warm hospitality, and unparalleled service in Cebu for over two decades. Not only do we serve elite-grade blends, but we also prepare dishes showcasing the Café’s relationship with local farms and purveyors.
